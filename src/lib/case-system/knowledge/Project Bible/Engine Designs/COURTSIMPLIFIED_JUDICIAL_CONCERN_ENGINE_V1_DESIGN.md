# COURTSIMPLIFIED JUDICIAL CONCERN ENGINE V1

STATUS: DESIGN PHASE

OWNER

CourtSimplified Cognitive Layer

PURPOSE

The Judicial Concern Engine identifies concerns likely to exist from the court's perspective.

The purpose is not to predict outcomes.

The purpose is to identify issues that may influence judicial decision-making.

---

PRIMARY QUESTIONS

What concerns would a judge likely have?

What information is missing?

What evidence appears weak?

What credibility concerns exist?

What procedural concerns exist?

What fairness concerns exist?

What public interest concerns exist?

---

LONG TERM GOAL

The Judicial Concern Engine becomes the court-perspective layer of CourtSimplified.

All major litigation reasoning should pass through this engine before strategy generation.

No duplicate judicial concern systems should be created.

This engine becomes the single source of truth for judicial concern analysis.
