# COURTSIMPLIFIED EVIDENCE GAP ENGINE V1

STATUS: DESIGN PHASE

OWNER

CourtSimplified Cognitive Layer

PURPOSE

The Evidence Gap Engine identifies missing evidence, weak evidence, incomplete proof, and documents or witnesses needed to strengthen a case.

The purpose is not to decide the case.

The purpose is to identify what is missing before the user relies on an incomplete case theory.

---

WHY THIS ENGINE EXISTS

Self-represented litigants often know what happened but do not know what evidence is needed to prove it.

This engine helps detect:

- missing documents
- missing witnesses
- missing dates
- missing amounts
- missing communications
- missing procedural proof
- missing damages proof
- missing authority support

---

PRIMARY QUESTIONS

What facts need proof?

What evidence currently supports each fact?

What evidence is missing?

What documents should the user look for?

What witnesses may matter?

What gaps create litigation risk?

What gaps create judge concerns?

LONG TERM GOAL

The Evidence Gap Engine becomes the primary missing-proof detection system for CourtSimplified.

It should help users understand:

- what they have
- what they do not have
- what they need
- why it matters
- what to do next

No duplicate evidence-gap systems should be created.

This engine becomes the single source of truth for missing evidence and proof gap analysis.
