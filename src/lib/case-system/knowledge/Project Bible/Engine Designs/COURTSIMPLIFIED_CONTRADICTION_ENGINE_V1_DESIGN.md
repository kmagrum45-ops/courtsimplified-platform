# COURTSIMPLIFIED CONTRADICTION ENGINE V1

STATUS: DESIGN PHASE

OWNER

CourtSimplified Cognitive Layer

PURPOSE

The Contradiction Engine identifies conflicts, inconsistencies, and incompatible positions within litigation materials.

The purpose is not to determine who is correct.

The purpose is to identify contradictions that may impact:

- credibility
- proof
- strategy
- procedure
- judicial concerns

---

WHY THIS ENGINE EXISTS

Contradictions are often among the most important facts in litigation.

Contradictions can reveal:

- credibility issues
- evidentiary weaknesses
- procedural risks
- unsupported allegations
- changing positions

This engine helps identify those risks early.

CONTRADICTION CATEGORIES

Category 1

Statement Contradictions

Examples:

- position changed
- explanation changed
- allegation changed
- admission conflicts with later statement

---

Category 2

Document Contradictions

Examples:

- document conflicts with testimony
- document conflicts with timeline
- document conflicts with claim

---

Category 3

Timeline Contradictions

Examples:

- date conflicts
- sequence conflicts
- event conflicts
- chronology conflicts

---

Category 4

Behavior Contradictions

Examples:

- actions inconsistent with claim
- conduct inconsistent with stated concern
- conduct inconsistent with requested relief

---

Category 5

Procedural Contradictions

Examples:

- procedural position changes
- inconsistent court positions
- inconsistent filing positions
- inconsistent service positions

- conflicting documents
- conflicting timelines
