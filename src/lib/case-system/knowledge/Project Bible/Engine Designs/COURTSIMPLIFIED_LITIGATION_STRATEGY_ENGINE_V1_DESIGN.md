# COURTSIMPLIFIED LITIGATION STRATEGY ENGINE V1

STATUS: DESIGN PHASE

OWNER

CourtSimplified Cognitive Layer

PURPOSE

The Litigation Strategy Engine converts legal analysis into actionable litigation strategy.

This engine does not provide legal advice.

Its purpose is to identify:

- strengths
- weaknesses
- opportunities
- risks
- priorities
- recommended next actions

based on the evidence, proof, procedure, credibility, and authority available within a case.

---

WHY THIS ENGINE EXISTS

Many litigants understand what happened but do not understand:

- what matters most
- where their case is strongest
- where their case is weakest
- what should happen next
- what risks require immediate attention

This engine helps convert litigation information into litigation direction.
